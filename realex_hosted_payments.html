RealAuth – Hosted Payment Page

About

The Hosted Payment Page (HPP) is a simple and straightforward way of taking payments on your website. In addition to processing card payments, HPP also allows you to accept transactions from alternative payment methods. The HPP integration method is suitable for merchants that do not have their own secure server. Using this method, the customer will be redirected to Realex Payments secure server to enter their details and complete the transaction. Because the customer is not entering any sensitive information on your own site, HPP removes many of your PCI obligations.

RealAuth Hosted Payment Page Features List

  • The merchant does not need a secure server.
  • The merchant’s website can be hosted anywhere, and on any platform.
  • The merchant does not collect their customer’s payment details, and does not have access to them.
  • The customer is redirected to the RealAuth application on a secure Realex Payments server by a script on the merchant’s website.
  • Realex Payments host a secure, merchant-branded web page. They collect the payment details and process the payment. The customer and the results of the transaction are sent back to the merchant via another script on the merchant’s web site.

Prerequisites for Integration with HPP

In order to create a merchant account for the Hosted Payment Page, a merchant must provide:

  • A Realex Payments Merchant ID
  • A sub-account name
  • Referring and response URLs
  • Bank merchant number (needed before your account can be set live)
  • Shopping cart used on your website (if applicable)
  • Any additional services (RealMPI, Alternative Payment Methods, DCC, RealVault)

Installation:

You may safely upload the following files to your instalation with no worry of overwriting anything:
  • ext/modules/payment/realauth/redirect.php
  • includes/modules/payment/realauth.php
  • inludes/languages/*english/modules/payment/realauth.php

Follow the next steps to complete install

Navigate to your admin's Modules->Payment Click "Install Module" button. Choose "Realex - RealAuth – Hosted Payment Page". Click "Install Module" button. Configure options and save.

You will need to have realex whitelist these files:
Response URL www.your-domain.com/ext/modules/payment/realauth/redirect.php

Referring URL www.your-domain.com/checkout_confirmation.php

You may set module to live or test account. It is recommended to first do a test transaction using your realex sandbox account. Test credit card numbers may be found at: https://resourcecentre.realexpayments.com/downloads.html?id=205
